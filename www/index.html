Safari Feed

What's Safari Feed

Safari feed gives you real-time updates of what is happening in the San Diego Zoo. Submit your own updates to let others know where the action is.

Adding Updates

Click the comment button in the top right. Write a brief note and submit the update with either the postive, , or negative, button depending on your message.

  • The lions are feeding!
  • The monkey exhibit is closed.

History

Click the layers button in the top right corner of the map, and select "History", to see historic activity heat map for your current time
